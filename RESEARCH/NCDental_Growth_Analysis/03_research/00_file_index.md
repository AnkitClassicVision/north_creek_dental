## Key Findings Summary

### Market Opportunity
- U.S. dental implant market: $2.1B (2024), growing 6-9% CAGR
- All-on-4 segment: Estimated 10-15% CAGR
- Local market: Affluent, aging (median age 42-45, income $100k+)
- SAM for All-on-4: $2.9-9M annually in primary market

### Competitive Position
- Primary competitor: ClearChoice Chicago (B- BBB, organizational complaints)
- NCDental advantages: In-house specialists, local presence, personalized care
- Differentiation: Credentials (40+ year periodontist), award-winning

### Growth Strategy
- Marketing budget: $10-15k/month recommended
- Primary channel: Google Ads (60% of budget)
- Expected ROI: 10-30x on marketing investment
- Key lever: Consultation-to-treatment conversion optimization

### Target Personas
- All-on-4: 65-72 retirees, 52-60 life-changers, 58-65 practical buyers, 45-55 anxious patients
- Cosmetic: 32-42 career women, 25-35 brides, 35-50 professional men

### Risks
- Economic downturn affecting elective procedures
- Rising Google Ads costs
- ClearChoice competitive response
- Key doctor departure

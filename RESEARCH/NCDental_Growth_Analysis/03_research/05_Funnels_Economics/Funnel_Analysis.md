# Funnel & Economics Analysis: NCDental Growth Strategy

## Executive Summary

High-ticket dental services require optimized funnels with multiple touchpoints. Based on industry benchmarks, NCDental's growth opportunity lies in improving consultation conversion rates and implementing systematic follow-up processes.

## Conversion Optimization Opportunities

### Lead → Consultation Conversion

**Current Industry Problems:**
- Slow response time (>5 min = 80% drop in conversion)
- Poor phone skills
- No online scheduling
- Lack of follow-up system

**Optimization Tactics:**
1. **Speed to Lead**
   - Answer calls within 3 rings
   - Respond to web forms within 5 minutes
   - Consider AI chat for after-hours

2. **Phone Script Optimization**
   - Train on handling price objections
   - Focus on getting consultation booked
   - Qualify but don't disqualify prematurely

3. **Online Scheduling**
   - Self-service booking option
   - Reduce friction in process

4. **Follow-Up Sequence**
   - Day 0: Initial contact
   - Day 1: Follow-up call if no booking
   - Day 3: Email with value content
   - Day 7: Final follow-up attempt
   - Day 14: Nurture sequence begins

### Consultation → Treatment Conversion

**Industry Benchmark:** 50-70% for All-on-4

**Optimization Tactics:**
1. **Same-Day Financing Decision**
   - Pre-qualify before consultation
   - Present payment options during consult
   - Have financing coordinator available

2. **Visual Aids & Technology**
   - 3D imaging review
   - Digital smile design preview
   - Before/after case gallery

3. **Objection Handling**
   - Price: Focus on financing, lifetime value
   - Fear: Sedation options, success rates
   - Time: Same-day procedures, efficient scheduling

4. **Follow-Up Protocol**
   - Day 1: Thank you + summary email
   - Day 3: Check-in call
   - Day 7: Financing reminder if applicable
   - Day 14: Limited-time consultation extension
   - Day 30+: Nurture sequence

## Financing as Conversion Lever

### Impact of Financing [HIGH CONFIDENCE]

Research indicates financing can increase high-ticket dental conversion by **25-40%**.

**Financing Options to Offer:**
1. **CareCredit** - Industry standard, 0% promotional periods
2. **Lending Club** - Extended terms available
3. **In-House Financing** - For qualified patients
4. **Flexible Payment Plans** - Staged treatment with payments

**Presentation Best Practices:**
- Present monthly payment alongside total price
- Example: "$35,000 or as low as $650/month for 60 months"
- Pre-qualify before consultation to reduce friction
- Make financing feel like a normal option, not "for people who can't afford it"

# Marketing Strategy: NCDental Growth Plan

## Executive Summary

NCDental's marketing strategy should focus on **Google Ads for high-intent capture**, **local SEO for sustained organic growth**, and **social media for cosmetic services**. The primary differentiator is credentials and personalized care vs. DSO "factory" model.

---

## Strategic Positioning

### Brand Positioning Statement

> For Chicago Southland adults seeking premium dental implant and cosmetic solutions, North Creek Dental Care offers board-certified specialist expertise and personalized care that delivers life-changing results—unlike volume-focused dental chains that prioritize throughput over individual outcomes.

### Key Differentiators (Messaging Pillars)

1. **Board-Certified Specialists**
   - Periodontist with 40+ years (former Northwestern Director)
   - Prosthodontist with implant fellowship
   - "Not just dentists—dental specialists"

2. **In-House Comprehensive Care**
   - No referrals to outside specialists
   - Seamless treatment from consultation to final restoration
   - One team, one location, one relationship

3. **Award-Winning Local Practice**
   - "Southland's Best Dental Office 2024"
   - Part of the community, not a national chain
   - Building trust one patient at a time

4. **Personalized Attention**
   - Treatment plans tailored to you
   - Direct access to your treating doctor
   - Not a number—a patient

## Channel Strategy

### Tier 1: High-Intent Capture (60% of Budget)

#### Google Ads - Search

**Budget Allocation:** $6,000-8,000/month

**Campaign Structure:**

**Campaign 1: All-on-4 / Full Arch**
- Keywords: "teeth in a day Tinley Park", "all on 4 dental implants Chicago", "full mouth implants near me", "dental implant specialist Chicago southland"
- Negative keywords: "cost", "free", "cheap" (for quality leads)
- Landing page: Dedicated All-on-4 page with form + phone
- Target CPA: $100-150/lead

**Campaign 2: Dental Implants (General)**
- Keywords: "dental implants Tinley Park", "implant dentist Orland Park", "missing teeth replacement"
- Landing page: General implants page
- Target CPA: $75-100/lead

**Campaign 3: Cosmetic Dentistry**
- Keywords: "veneers Tinley Park", "cosmetic dentist Orland Park", "porcelain veneers Chicago"
- Landing page: Cosmetic services page
- Target CPA: $50-75/lead

**Campaign 4: Brand Defense**
- Keywords: "North Creek Dental", "NCDental", brand misspellings
- Low budget, high priority
- Ensure competitors don't capture branded searches

### Tier 2: Local SEO & Organic (20% of Budget)

**Budget Allocation:** $2,000-3,000/month (agency + tools)

**Google Business Profile Optimization:**
- Complete all fields
- Weekly posts with photos/offers
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- Add Q&A content
- Service area optimization

**Local SEO Priorities:**
1. "Dental implants Tinley Park" - Target #1-3
2. "All on 4 Orland Park" - Target #1-3
3. "Cosmetic dentist Chicago Southland" - Target top 5
4. "Teeth in a day Illinois" - Target top 5

**Content Strategy:**
- Monthly blog post on implant/cosmetic topics
- Location-specific service pages
- FAQ schema markup
- Before/after gallery with alt text

**Citations & Directories:**
- Healthgrades, Zocdoc, Vitals (complete profiles)
- Yelp (claimed, optimized)
- Local directories (Tinley Park Chamber, etc.)

### Tier 3: Social Media & Retargeting (20% of Budget)

**Budget Allocation:** $2,000-3,000/month

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Retargeting website visitors (highest ROI)
- Before/after transformation videos
- Patient testimonial snippets
- Targeting: 45-70, 25-mile radius, interests in dental/health

**Organic Social:**
- Instagram: Weekly before/after posts, stories
- Facebook: Patient stories, practice updates, educational content
- LinkedIn: Dr. profiles, B2B networking for referrals

**YouTube:**
- Patient testimonial videos
- Procedure explanation videos
- "What to expect" content
- Dr. introductions

## Creative Strategy

### Messaging by Persona

**Retiring Robert (Expertise Focus):**
> "Board-certified specialists with 40+ years combined experience. Your smile deserves nothing less."

**Desperate Diane (Transformation Focus):**
> "Ready for a fresh start? Flexible financing makes your dream smile affordable. See real patient transformations."

**Practical Paul (Efficiency Focus):**
> "Walk in with failing teeth. Walk out with a new smile. Same day. All-inclusive pricing."

**Anxious Angela (Comfort Focus):**
> "Dental anxiety shouldn't stop you from smiling. Gentle sedation. Judgment-free care. Expert results."

### Ad Creative Types

1. **Testimonial Videos** (30-60 sec)
   - Real patients sharing transformation stories
   - Before/after visuals
   - Emotional impact + practical benefits

2. **Before/After Carousels** (Facebook/Instagram)
   - Multiple transformation examples
   - Case-appropriate for each persona
   - CTA to free consultation

3. **Expert Positioning** (Search ads, display)
   - Credentials highlighted
   - Award mentions
   - Trust signals (years experience, cases completed)

4. **Urgency/Offer** (Seasonal)
   - "Complimentary consultation through [date]"
   - "Limited All-on-4 appointments this month"
   - End-of-year insurance/HSA messaging

# Partnership Strategy: NCDental Referral & Alliance Network

## Executive Summary

Strategic partnerships can provide high-quality, low-cost patient referrals and strengthen NCDental's market position. Priority partnerships include general dentists, medical specialists, and financing partners.

---

## Partnership Categories

### Category 1: Professional Referral Partners

#### General Dentists (Highest Priority)

**Value Proposition:**
- NCDental handles complex implant cases, returns patients for ongoing care
- No competition for bread-and-butter dentistry
- Reliable, quality outcomes reflect well on referring dentist

**Target Partners:**
- General dentists within 15-mile radius who don't place implants
- Newer dentists building practices who need specialist support
- Established practices without in-house implant capability

**Partnership Model:**
- Formal referral agreement
- Streamlined referral process (dedicated phone line/form)
- Regular case updates to referring doctor
- CE lunch-and-learn events
- Return patients after implant phase for restorative care (when appropriate)

**Outreach Strategy:**
1. Identify 50+ GP practices in service area
2. Send introduction letter with case study
3. Follow up with office visit or lunch meeting
4. Offer to present CE course on implants
5. Quarterly check-in on referred patients

**Expected Yield:**
- 20-30 active referring dentists
- 2-4 referrals/month per active partner
- 40-120 implant referrals/year
- Conversion rate: 60-70% (pre-qualified)

#### Oral Surgeons (Collaborative)

**Situation:**
- Some oral surgeons only place implants, don't restore
- Opportunity for mutual referral (surgery â†” restoration)

**Partnership Model:**
- NCDental restores implants placed elsewhere
- NCDental refers surgical extraction cases if needed
- Co-management of complex cases

#### Prosthodontists (Network)

**Situation:**
- Limited prosthodontists in Southland
- NCDental has Dr. Kang (competitive advantage)

**Partnership Model:**
- Position as the prosthodontic center for the region
- Accept referrals from other practices for complex restorative
- Build reputation as "the place for difficult cases"

### Category 2: Medical Referral Partners

#### Primary Care Physicians

**Value Proposition:**
- Oral health connected to systemic health
- Diabetic patients need dental clearance
- Sleep apnea screening and referral

**Target Partners:**
- Internal medicine practices
- Family medicine groups
- Geriatric medicine specialists

**Partnership Approach:**
- Educational materials on oral-systemic health
- Referral pads for dental screening
- Offer to speak at practice meetings

#### ENT / Sleep Medicine Specialists

**Value Proposition:**
- Sleep apnea oral appliances
- Snoring treatment
- Cross-referral for related conditions

**Partnership Model:**
- Receive referrals for dental sleep medicine
- Refer surgical candidates back
- Co-marketing opportunities

#### Oncologists / Cancer Centers

**Value Proposition:**
- Pre-radiation dental clearance
- Implant reconstruction post-cancer treatment
- Specialized care for immunocompromised patients

**Partnership Approach:**
- Connect with local cancer centers
- Position as experienced with complex medical cases
- Offer priority scheduling for cancer patients

### Category 3: Financial Partners

#### CareCredit / Lending Club

**Current Status:** Likely already in place

**Optimization:**
- Ensure staff is trained on financing presentation
- Pre-approval process streamlined
- Marketing co-op dollars available?

#### In-House Financing

**Opportunity:**
- Offer NCDental payment plans for qualified patients
- Reduces third-party fees
- Builds patient loyalty

**Considerations:**
- Credit risk assessment
- Collection process
- Legal/compliance requirements

### Category 4: Corporate & Employer Partners

## Implications for NCDental

### SO WHAT? (Significance)
Referral partnerships provide the highest-quality leads with lowest acquisition cost. A strong referral network creates sustainable competitive advantage.

### NOW WHAT? (Action Items)
1. **Week 1:** Create target GP list, draft outreach materials
2. **Week 2-4:** Begin outreach to top 20 prospects
3. **Month 2:** Schedule first CE event
4. **Month 3:** Establish formal referral tracking
5. **Ongoing:** Monthly partner communication, quarterly recognition

### WHAT IF? (Scenarios)
- **Strong GP network:** 40-50% of implant cases from referrals, lowest CAC
- **Weak GP network:** Rely heavily on paid marketing, higher CAC
- **Medical partnerships:** Opens new patient populations, longer sales cycle

### COMPARED TO? (Benchmarks)
- Top implant practices: 30-50% of cases from referrals
- Referral patient close rate: 70-80% (vs. 50-60% for marketing leads)
- Cost per referral: Near $0 (relationship investment only)

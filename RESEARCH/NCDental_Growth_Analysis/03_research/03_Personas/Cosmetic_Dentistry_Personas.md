# Patient Personas: Cosmetic Dentistry (Veneers & Smile Enhancement)

## Persona Overview

Based on cosmetic dentistry market research and local demographics, we've identified 3 primary personas for cosmetic services in the Chicago Southland market.

---

## Persona 1: "Career-Focused Carla"

### Demographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Age** | 32-42 |
| **Gender** | Female |
| **Location** | Tinley Park / Orland Park |
| **Occupation** | Sales executive / Manager / Professional |
| **Household Income** | $120,000-$180,000 |
| **Insurance** | Employer dental (no cosmetic coverage) |
| **Family** | Married or single, 0-2 children |

### Psychographics
- **Motivation:** Professional image and confidence
- **Values:** Investment in self, quality over price
- **Research Style:** Instagram, before/after galleries, reviews
- **Decision Timeline:** 1-2 months

### Current Situation
- Works in client-facing role
- Successful but self-conscious about smile in presentations
- Has minor imperfections (chips, discoloration, slight gaps)
- Sees smile as limiting career advancement

### Pain Points
1. Self-conscious on video calls and in meetings
2. Avoids smiling in professional photos
3. Compares herself to colleagues with "perfect" smiles
4. Worried about looking too "fake" or overdone

### Decision Factors (Ranked)
1. **Natural-looking results** (35%)
2. **Before/after portfolio** (25%)
3. **Minimal downtime** (20%)
4. **Premium quality materials** (20%)

### Objections to Overcome
- "Will it look natural?"
- "How long will I need to be out of work?"
- "What if I don't like the results?"

### Messaging That Resonates
- "Natural-looking veneers designed for your face"
- "Minimal prep veneers - preserve your tooth structure"
- "Digital smile design - preview your results first"
- "Return to work the next day"

### Typical Procedure Value
- Veneers (6-10 teeth): $8,000-$15,000
- Whitening add-on: $500-800
- **Average Case: $10,000-$16,000**

### Acquisition Channels
1. Instagram (cosmetic dentistry hashtags)
2. Google Search: "porcelain veneers Tinley Park"
3. Referral from friends/colleagues
4. Facebook retargeting

## Persona 2: "Special Event Sarah"

### Demographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Age** | 25-35 |
| **Gender** | Female |
| **Location** | Homer Glen / Frankfort |
| **Occupation** | Various (office, retail, healthcare) |
| **Household Income** | $75,000-$120,000 |
| **Insurance** | Limited dental coverage |
| **Family** | Engaged or newlywed, no children |

### Psychographics
- **Motivation:** Major life event (wedding, milestone birthday)
- **Values:** Looking her best for photos, social media
- **Research Style:** Pinterest, Instagram, wedding blogs
- **Decision Timeline:** 3-6 months before event

### Current Situation
- Engaged or planning milestone celebration
- Wants perfect smile for photos and memories
- Has been thinking about fixing teeth for years
- Wedding/event provides deadline and justification

### Pain Points
1. Doesn't want to regret smile in wedding photos
2. Budget is stretched with event expenses
3. Concerned about timing and healing
4. Overwhelmed by options (veneers vs. whitening vs. bonding)

### Decision Factors (Ranked)
1. **Timing/scheduling flexibility** (30%)
2. **Financing options** (30%)
3. **Range of options at different price points** (25%)
4. **Before/after similar cases** (15%)

### Objections to Overcome
- "Can we get this done before my wedding date?"
- "What can I do within my budget?"
- "What if it doesn't heal in time?"

### Messaging That Resonates
- "Your dream smile for your dream day"
- "Smile makeover packages for brides"
- "Flexible financing - start for as low as $XXX/month"
- "Free smile consultation - explore your options"

### Typical Procedure Value
- Full veneers (8-10): $12,000-$18,000
- Whitening only: $500-800
- Bonding (minor): $1,500-3,000
- **Average Case: $5,000-$15,000**

### Acquisition Channels
1. Instagram (wedding hashtags, bridal content)
2. Pinterest (wedding smile pins)
3. Google Search: "wedding smile makeover"
4. Bridal show/event marketing
5. Partnership with wedding planners

## Persona 3: "Confident Connor"

### Demographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Age** | 35-50 |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Location** | Orland Park / Mokena |
| **Occupation** | Business owner / Sales / Executive |
| **Household Income** | $150,000+ |
| **Insurance** | Various |
| **Family** | Married, children |

### Psychographics
- **Motivation:** Competitive advantage, first impressions
- **Values:** Quality, efficiency, results
- **Research Style:** Direct research, values credentials
- **Decision Timeline:** 1-2 months once decided

### Current Situation
- Successful but aware smile isn't projecting success
- Has worn, stained, or damaged teeth from years of neglect
- Wife has encouraged cosmetic improvement
- Sees competitors/colleagues with better smiles

### Pain Points
1. Teeth show age/wear that doesn't match self-image
2. First impression concerns in business settings
3. Limited time for extensive procedures
4. Wants quality without extensive research

### Decision Factors (Ranked)
1. **Efficiency/minimal appointments** (35%)
2. **Quality/durability** (30%)
3. **Masculine, natural results** (20%)
4. **Doctor credibility** (15%)

### Objections to Overcome
- "How many appointments will this take?"
- "Will this look natural for a man?"
- "What's the best option - give me your recommendation"

### Messaging That Resonates
- "Executive smile makeovers - minimal time, maximum impact"
- "Premium materials built to last"
- "Trusted by Southland business leaders"
- "Complimentary executive consultation"

### Typical Procedure Value
- Veneers (6-8): $8,000-$14,000
- Crown replacements: $2,000-$4,000
- Whitening: $500-800
- **Average Case: $8,000-$15,000**

### Acquisition Channels
1. LinkedIn (professional image content)
2. Google Search: "cosmetic dentist for men"
3. Referral from other business owners
4. Golf club/professional association marketing

## Cosmetic Service Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Offerings

**Tier 1: Entry Level ($500-$2,000)**
- Professional whitening
- Minor bonding (1-2 teeth)
- Gateway to larger treatments

**Tier 2: Enhancement ($3,000-$8,000)**
- Bonding package (4-6 teeth)
- Partial veneer treatment (4 teeth)
- Combined whitening + bonding

**Tier 3: Transformation ($8,000-$20,000)**
- Full veneer smile makeover (8-10 teeth)
- Comprehensive cosmetic treatment
- Premium materials and design

## Implications for NCDental

### SO WHAT? (Significance)
Cosmetic patients are highly visual and emotional buyers. Portfolio quality and social proof matter more than credentials for this segment.

### NOW WHAT? (Action Items)
1. Build extensive before/after photo gallery
2. Invest in social media presence (especially Instagram)
3. Create bride/event-specific marketing campaign
4. Develop male-focused cosmetic content
5. Implement digital smile design for consultations

### WHAT IF? (Scenarios)
- **Strong Instagram presence:** Attracts younger demographic, builds brand
- **Neglect social media:** Miss large segment of cosmetic prospects
- **Male marketing focus:** Underserved market, less competition

### COMPARED TO? (Benchmarks)
- Top cosmetic practices: 50%+ of leads from Instagram
- Industry average cosmetic case: $5,000-$15,000
- Male cosmetic market: Growing 15%+ annually

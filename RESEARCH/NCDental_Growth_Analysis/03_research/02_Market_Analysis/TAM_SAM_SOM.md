# Market Analysis: TAM, SAM, SOM

## Executive Summary

The dental implant and cosmetic dentistry markets present significant growth opportunities for North Creek Dental Care. The All-on-4/full-arch segment is growing faster than the overall implant market, and NCDental's geographic location in affluent Chicago Southland positions it well to capture market share.

## Key Growth Drivers

### Demand-Side Drivers

1. **Aging Demographics** [HIGH CONFIDENCE]
   - Chicago Southland has above-average 65+ population (17-22%)
   - Baby boomers prefer fixed solutions over dentures
   - 178M Americans missing at least one tooth; 40M+ edentulous

2. **Rising Awareness** [MEDIUM CONFIDENCE]
   - Social media driving cosmetic dentistry demand
   - All-on-4 marketing by ClearChoice and DSOs increasing awareness
   - Patient willingness to travel for quality implant care

3. **Income Profile** [HIGH CONFIDENCE]
   - Median HH income $100k+ in primary market
   - Sufficient discretionary income for $20k-$50k procedures
   - Lower price sensitivity than average U.S. market

### Supply-Side Drivers

1. **Technology Adoption**
   - 3D CBCT imaging improving outcomes
   - Digital workflows reducing chair time
   - Immediate-load protocols expanding All-on-4 appeal

2. **Financing Availability**
   - CareCredit and lending partners
   - Extended payment plans making high-ticket procedures accessible

## Implications for NCDental

### SO WHAT? (Significance)
NCDental operates in a **high-income, aging market** with strong latent demand for All-on-4 and cosmetic services. The combination of experienced specialists, in-house comprehensive care, and local trust positions it to capture meaningful market share.

### NOW WHAT? (Action Items)
1. **Focus marketing on 55-70 age demographic** for All-on-4
2. **Target 35-50 professionals** for cosmetic services
3. **Emphasize financing options** to unlock price-sensitive qualified leads
4. **Differentiate from ClearChoice** on credentials and personalized care

### WHAT IF? (Scenarios)
- **If trend continues:** All-on-4 market doubles by 2030; early market share gains compound
- **If trend reverses:** Economic downturn may delay elective procedures; financing becomes more critical
- **If no action taken:** ClearChoice and DSOs capture awareness; local practices lose share

### COMPARED TO? (Benchmarks)
- Industry average consultation conversion: 50-70%
- Top performers: 70%+ conversion with strong follow-up
- ClearChoice: High volume, B- BBB rating, organizational complaints
- NCDental current: Unknown (establish baseline)

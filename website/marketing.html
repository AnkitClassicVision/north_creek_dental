Growth Plan

Marketing Strategy

Channel allocation, budget recommendations, and persona-specific messaging for patient acquisition.

Recommended Budget Allocation

Monthly Investment: $10,000 - $15,000

Google Ads (Paid Search) 60% - $6,000-$9,000
SEO & Content 20% - $2,000-$3,000
Social Media (Paid) 10% - $1,000-$1,500
Referral Program 10% - $1,000-$1,500

Channel Rationale

  • Google Ads (60%)

    Highest-intent channel. Patients actively searching for implant solutions. Direct ROI tracking.

  • SEO & Content (20%)

    Long-term asset building. Educational content for research-phase patients. Local SEO dominance.

  • Social Media (10%)

    Facebook/Instagram for cosmetic personas. Retargeting and brand awareness.

  • Referral Program (10%)

    GP referral network development. Patient referral incentives. Lowest CAC channel.

Healthcare Marketing Compliance

All marketing materials must comply with applicable dental advertising regulations, state dental board guidelines, and HIPAA requirements. Patient testimonials require written consent. Before/after photos must accurately represent typical results with appropriate disclaimers. Claims about outcomes must be substantiated. Consult with legal and compliance professionals before implementing advertising campaigns.

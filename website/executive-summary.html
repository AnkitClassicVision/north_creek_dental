  • Tinley Park median income: $103,800

Strategic Recommendations

Now What

  • 1. Implement $10-15K/month marketing budget with 60% to Google Ads
  • 2. Deploy persona-specific landing pages and messaging
  • 3. Add third-party financing options (CareCredit, Proceed)
  • 4. Optimize consultation-to-treatment conversion process
  • 5. Build GP referral network with CE credit offerings

What If

  • 1. Economic downturn: Emphasize financing, target stable-income personas
  • 2. ClearChoice aggressive expansion: Double down on personalization differentiator
  • 3. Google Ads CPL spikes: Shift budget to SEO and referral channels
  • 4. Specialist retirement: Implement succession planning, knowledge transfer

Risk Assessment Summary

Key Assumptions Challenged

Market Size Accuracy
Local SAM estimates may vary +/- 30% based on actual implant procedure rates in Chicago Southland.
Trust vs. Price Balance
Economic conditions may shift patient priorities toward price sensitivity during downturns.
Conversion Rates
50-70% benchmark assumes optimized consultation process and financing availability.

Mitigation Strategies

Diversified Marketing
Multi-channel approach (Google, SEO, referrals) reduces dependency on any single source.
Financing Options
Third-party financing addresses price sensitivity without reducing case value.
Succession Planning
Document specialist expertise and build team depth for long-term continuity.

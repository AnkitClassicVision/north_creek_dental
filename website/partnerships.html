GP Referral Network Development

Value Proposition for GPs

  • Patients return for routine care - No patient loss
  • Free CE credits - Lunch & learn sessions
  • Clear communication - Treatment updates & reports
  • Referral simplicity - Direct line, fast scheduling
  • Confidence in outcomes - 40+ year specialist

Referral Program Structure

  • Referral Coordinator

    Dedicated staff member managing GP relationships and referral follow-up.

  • Referral Portal

    Simple online form or dedicated phone line for easy patient referral.

  • 48-Hour Feedback

    Referring GP receives consultation summary within 2 business days.

  • Co-Treatment Reports

    Full treatment documentation shared for patient continuity.

GP Outreach Implementation

Month 1-2: Foundation
  • - Identify 50 target GPs within 10-mile radius
  • - Create referral materials (brochures, digital assets)
  • - Develop CE presentation on implant indications
Month 3-4: Initial Outreach
  • - Personal visits to top 20 priority practices
  • - Schedule first lunch & learn sessions
  • - Launch referral tracking system
Month 5-6: Expansion
  • - Expand to remaining 30 practices
  • - Host quarterly CE event (accredited)
  • - Implement feedback loop with active referrers
Ongoing: Nurture
  • - Monthly newsletter to GP network
  • - Quarterly appreciation events
  • - Annual referrer recognition program

Corporate & Community Partnerships

Corporate Wellness

Partner with local employers offering dental benefits for executive wellness programs.

  • On-site screening days
  • Employee discount programs
  • Lunch & learn presentations

Senior Communities

Target active adult communities and retirement centers in the Southland area.

  • Educational seminars
  • Health fair participation
  • Resident group discounts

Community Events

Build brand awareness through local community involvement and sponsorships.

  • Local charity sponsorships
  • Chamber of Commerce
  • Youth sports sponsorships

Patient Referral Program

Program Structure

Referrer Reward

$100-250 credit per successful referral

Applied to future treatment or redeemable as gift card

New Patient Bonus

$50-100 credit toward treatment

Incentivizes referred patients to schedule

Tracking

Simple referral cards + digital submission option. CRM tracking for attribution.

Implementation Tactics

  • Post-treatment ask during positive moments
  • Follow-up email with referral link
  • Anniversary reminder emails
  • Social sharing encouragement (with consent)
  • "Thank you" recognition program

CAC = Customer Acquisition Cost. LTV = Lifetime Value. Referral channels consistently outperform paid acquisition in both cost efficiency and patient quality.

Strategic Insight: Referrals as Competitive Moat

While DSOs compete on advertising spend, a robust referral network creates a sustainable competitive advantage that cannot be easily replicated. GP relationships built on trust and patient outcomes compound over time.

Partnership Program Disclaimer

Referral incentive programs must comply with applicable state dental board regulations and anti-kickback statutes. Consult with legal counsel before implementing any patient or professional referral incentive programs. All partnership arrangements should be documented in writing with appropriate compliance review.

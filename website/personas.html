Patient Personas

7 detailed patient profiles representing ideal candidates for All-on-4 implants (4 personas) and cosmetic dentistry services (3 personas).

All-on-4 Implant Personas

Retiring Robert

"I want to enjoy my retirement with a smile I'm proud of"

Male, 62-70, Married
Retiring executive/professional
$150K+ income, substantial savings
Orland Park, Tinley Park suburbs
Pain Points
  • Dentures affecting enjoyment of food
  • Self-conscious about smile in photos
  • Worried about appearing "old"
Decision Factors
  • Specialist credentials (trust)
  • Permanent solution vs. dentures
  • Experience and proven results
Key Message

"Enjoy your retirement with teeth that let you eat, laugh, and smile with confidence."

Desperate Diane

"I need this fixed now - I can't live like this anymore"

Female, 50-60, Various status
Teacher, admin, service professional
$60-90K, needs financing
Broader Chicago Southland
Pain Points
  • Severe dental issues causing daily pain
  • Social isolation due to embarrassment
  • Failed previous dental work
Decision Factors
  • Financing availability (critical)
  • Speed of treatment
  • Compassionate, non-judgmental care
Key Message

"We understand your situation. Let us help you get your life back with flexible payment options."

Practical Paul

"Show me the numbers - I want the best long-term value"

Male, 55-65, Married
Engineer, accountant, analyst
$100-150K, values ROI
Orland Park, Mokena area
Pain Points
  • Tired of ongoing dental repairs
  • Calculates lifetime cost of dentures
  • Wants one-time permanent solution
Decision Factors
  • Total cost comparison analysis
  • Longevity and durability data
  • Success rate statistics
Key Message

"All-on-4 costs less over 20 years than repeated denture replacements. Here's the math."

Anxious Angela

"I'm terrified of the dentist, but I know I need help"

Female, 45-60, Various status
Healthcare, education, office work
$70-120K, will pay for comfort
Tinley Park, local community
Pain Points
  • Severe dental anxiety/phobia
  • History of traumatic dental experiences
  • Avoided dentist for years
Decision Factors
  • Sedation options available
  • Gentle, patient approach
  • Reviews from other anxious patients
Key Message

"We specialize in helping anxious patients. Sedation options ensure you'll be comfortable throughout."

$100K+, invests in appearance
Chicago suburbs, commuter
Pain Points

  • Competing with younger colleagues
Decision Factors

  • Premium quality materials

"I want to look perfect for my wedding photos"

Female, 25-40, Engaged/planning
Various professions
$60-100K, budget for wedding
Timeline-driven (event date)
Pain Points

  • Deadline pressure for event

Decision Factors

  • Payment plans available

Finance, tech, business owner
$120K+, spends on self-care
Urban/suburban, fitness-oriented
Pain Points

  • Dating profile/social media appearance
Decision Factors

  • Premium provider reputation
